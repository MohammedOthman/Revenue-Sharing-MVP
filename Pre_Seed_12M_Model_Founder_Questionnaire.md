## How to use this sheet

1. Fill the **🟢 Your answer** cells. Leave anything you don't know blank — a blank becomes a visible
   **🔴 [INPUT REQUIRED]** placeholder and the model still runs *symbolically* around it.
2. The **📊 Reference** column is **cited external guidance only**. It is **never** written into your model
   unless you explicitly adopt it — and if you adopt one, mark it (write `ADOPT` in the answer cell).
3. **Currency = SAR.** Convert any USD cost at the USD/SAR peg **~3.75** (confirm in A-note).
4. Three input tiers — every number ends up flagged as one of these:
   - **🟢 FOUNDER INPUT** — your answer.
   - **📊 REFERENCE** — a cited figure you *explicitly* adopted.
   - **🔴 INPUT REQUIRED** — unanswered placeholder.
5. **Keep four things distinct — do not conflate:** **bookings ≠ billings (invoiced) ≠ recognized revenue ≠ cash collected.** **VAT is working capital, never revenue.**
6. **Statutory/regulatory rates** (Part 3) are applied by default but flagged **"verify"** — they are law, not invented company data. Confirm before filing.

**Tip — answer these first (highest impact on runway):** A1 starting cash · A2/A3 raise · B1 founder cash salary + deferral · C headcount plan · E pricing/ACV/mix · F2 sales-cycle + F4 go-live lag · G1/G2 billing mix + DSO · the pilot→paid conversion % (Section E). These move the cash-out month the most.
