## Mohammed Othman — Founder & CEO
**محمد عثمان**

> *Achieving investment & commercial growth success — elevating growth beyond the numbers.*

Founder and Chief Executive of Reven. Builds and delivers growth strategies and execution
plans for enterprises, using financial analysis and practical growth strategy to maximize
the value of the entity. Core focus areas:

- Building **growth partnerships** for B2B businesses
- Building **value-added partnerships** for enterprise clients
- **Investment strategy** and value-creation plans
- **M&A** plans and deal sourcing (buy-side and sell-side)
- **Vertical growth** analysis — organic growth vs. M&A strategies

### Selected engagements

- **Delisting a ~$200M company (SAR ~1B project).** A cross-stakeholder transaction in the
  Saudi ecosystem — family offices, banks, private-equity firms, and the **Public Investment
  Fund (PIF)** — to delist an undervalued company from the Swiss market (SIX) and pursue a
  TASI listing by 2028. Built the **undervaluation thesis**, ran apples-to-apples equity
  research against **8+ TASI and global peers**, advanced financial-ratio analysis, the
  post-listing **premium thesis / deal ROI**, Swiss vs. TASI macro and micro analysis, and
  the SIX-vs-TASI premium-realization comparison.
- **~SAR 50M acquisition (ongoing).** Built the **synergy quantification** (≈4× ROI in
  5 years), the acquirer's vertical growth strategy, the IPO-premium impact for the buy-side,
  global-comparables equity research, dynamic margin-synergy modeling, and sell-side
  valuation with pre- vs. post-acquisition financial and margin analysis. Stakeholders
  included Jadwa, Watheeq, Niceone, and مخازن العناية.
- **Enterprise growth strategy for a B2B SaaS.** Took a company with no clear market size,
  ICP, or growth path — and investors declining on an unjustified TAM — and redefined the
  market size and ICP, market-penetration tactics, a year-2 TAM, the GTM motion and
  scalability metrics, an API/digital growth strategy, a RevOps and growth engine, a
  redesigned investor pitch deck, and an enterprise sales motion with a built-out pipeline.
- **Late-stage VC investment firm.** Investment strategy and early-stage due-diligence
  approach in the **gaming** sector, vertical and industry-history analysis, emerging
  business-model assessment, and a value-creation roadmap for portfolio ventures.
- **Sector strategy & value creation** across **B2B PropTech** (PropTech 1.0 → 3.0,
  lead-management automation), **HealthTech** (5-year expansion, exit-strategy engineering,
  genome-bank strategy), **EV & battery services**, a **social-media app** (network effects,
  data monetization), and a **data company** (product-led motion, GTM metrics, VC due
  diligence, global revenue-stream benchmarking).

### What this means for Reven

Reven is a bet on owning the commercial and financial "system of record" between B2B
partners. The CEO pairs **investment-grade financial analysis** (valuation, synergy
modeling, equity research, M&A) with **hands-on GTM and growth execution** (ICP, TAM, RevOps,
enterprise sales) — the exact combination needed to set the phase model, defend its gates,
raise capital against a credible value-creation thesis, and convert the strategy into a
fundable, sellable product.

## Mohammed Masmali — Legal & Contracts
**محمد مسلمي** *(previously محمد عثمان مسلمي)*
[linkedin.com/in/-mohammed-masmali](https://www.linkedin.com/in/-mohammed-masmali)

### Approach
For Reven, the legal-technical dimension of contracts is a core mandate. He does not treat
contracts as mere clauses — he uses **reverse engineering** to understand the purpose and
impact of each clause, how it is applied, and what causes disputes. The aim is to manage the
**life of the contract** between parties — including diversifying the sources of notices and
obligations — so the contract binds the parties more tightly to **mutual success**. He has
consistently pushed to detail who is affected by a contract and how they intersect across
departments and decision-making, so that contract administration becomes a means of success
and protection for all parties. Having worked with government, quasi-government, and
institutional bodies — and seen companies fail for lack of certainty in how contracts are
enforced — he insists a contract must be drafted in a way that protects its parties and
preserves the rights at the heart of any enterprise.

### Selected projects
- **Legislation — Olympic & Paralympic Committee.** Contributed to drafting legislation,
  including a dispute-resolution regulation and discipline & appeals regulations. Analyzed
  close to **1,000 local and international regulations** to determine the optimal framework;
  reviewed the committee's founding statute and **97 Saudi federations**; reviewed **80+**
  disciplinary and appeals regulations and analyzed them legislatively; reviewed sports law
  and produced simplified summaries.
- **Legislation — Executive Offices Company.** Analyzed internal regulations, policies, and
  operational templates; reviewed crisis-related operational processes; reviewed and drafted
  **22+ internal policies**; executed the legislative transformation of procedures; and put
  in place governance of investigations to ensure compliance and regulatory adherence.
- **Labor Cases Management — Ministry of Human Resources and Social Development**
  (Jan 2020 – Feb 2021). Studied and managed labor cases for companies, institutions, and
  individuals to facilitate **risk forecasting** from decisions, analyzing the rulings that
  might be issued against the employer as a result of those decisions.
- **Endowment (Waqf) projects.** Coordinated the relationship between beneficiaries by
  drafting a comprehensive charter to ensure the endowment's sustainability and development —
  including managing three endowment properties and setting an independent vision for
  redevelopment and expansion.
- **Commercial contract redrafting.** Drafted and audited **300+ contracts** within a
  specialized legal-technical project, studying the impact of the new **Civil Transactions
  Law** on contractual clauses and providing the legal team with simplified explanations.

### Research & legislative writing
Legislative article *"Termination is not an administrative decision"*; legislative reading
*"Provisions of resignation"*; applied study *"The key rules required to study commercial
cases"*; plus practitioner guides — legislative & judicial drafting, policy review for
companies, internal investigations for companies, real-estate legislation, and contract review.

### What this means for Reven
Reven's strategy earns the right to become the **bilateral revenue-sharing system of record**.
That depends on contracts, claims, and disputes that hold up under scrutiny. Deep,
hands-on command of contract lifecycle, dispute resolution, compliance regimes, and
Saudi legislation directly de-risks the **Capture** and **Settle** phases — where
claim integrity, attribution, dispute workflow, and a defensible ledger-of-record are the
product.
